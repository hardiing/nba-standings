  • PPG

Data found in each table:


PPG: Player stats ordered by points per game, limited to top 10


RPG: Player stats ordered by rebounds per game, limited to top 10


APG: Player stats ordered by assists per game, limited to top 10


League Standings: conference rank, team, record, home record, road record, last 10 record,
PPG, opponent PPG, record against opponents over .500, and record post-all-star break, ordered by conference rank


About the project:


A flask application that collects data from a couple different NBA API endpoints and inserts them into a MySQL database. The HTML tables are built by querying the tables for the data most relevant to me.


Technologies used: Python, Flask, Docker, MySQL, HTML/CSS
